About the programme.

FutureScape 24 is a global initiative designed to inspire innovation, foster creativity, and encourage collaboration among students and educators worldwide. This unique program brings together young minds and experienced educators to tackle some of the most pressing challenges of our time, with a focus on sustainability, technology, entrepreneurship, and education.

FutureScape 24 features three cornerstone events:

  • InnoVision: An Ideathon designed for school students, where young innovators present their ideas to address contemporary issues and challenges.
  • Hackverse: A Hackathon for college students, challenging participants to develop cutting-edge technological solutions with real-world applications.
  • Conclave: A forum for educators from schools and colleges, focused on discussions around sustainable practices, education innovation, and the future of learning.

Participants will have the chance to compete for attractive cash prizes, gain global recognition, and contribute to shaping a better future. FutureScape 24 is more than just a competition; it’s a movement to inspire, innovate, and impact the world.

Join us in this exciting journey to explore new frontiers and make a difference.

Program Objective

  • Encourage participants to think critically and creatively about solutions to global challenges.
  • Foster the development of innovative ideas and technological solutions that can make a real-world impact.
  • Create opportunities for collaboration across different age groups and educational backgrounds, promoting the exchange of ideas and best practices.
  • Highlight the importance of sustainability and the role that education and technology can play in achieving global goals.
  • Provide a platform for educators to share and discuss their experiences, best practices, and innovative approaches to teaching.

About the Institution

CHRIST (Deemed to be University) emerged from the educational vision of St. Kuriakose Elias Chavara, a 19th-century educationist and social reformer in South India. The university is administered by the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI), the first Catholic indigenous congregation, founded by St. Kuriakose Elias Chavara in 1831. Established as Christ College in 1969, the institution has grown significantly, earning a reputation for academic excellence and expanding its presence across India.

In 1998, CHRIST (Deemed to be University) was accredited by NAAC, and in 2004, it was granted autonomy by the University Grants Commission (UGC). Recognized as an Institution with Potential for Excellence in 2006, it was declared a ‘Deemed to be University’ by the Ministry of Education in 2008. The Pune Lavasa Campus, inaugurated on July 20th, 2014, has quickly become a prominent center for higher education.

The Pune Lavasa Campus offers a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, with courses in disciplines such as Data Science, Economics, Business Administration, Commerce, Languages and Law. These programs are designed to foster a robust academic environment, encouraging in-depth study and innovation. Aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP), CHRIST Lavasa has implemented a flexible and interdisciplinary curriculum that emphasizes research, skill development, and holistic learning. This approach ensures that students are well-prepared to excel in a rapidly evolving global landscape.

Beyond academics, CHRIST Lavasa is a vibrant community where students can engage in over 30 active clubs and participate in international conferences, workshops, and events. These opportunities not only enhance personal growth but also broaden global perspectives.At CHRIST Lavasa, we are committed to nurturing responsible global citizens by combining academic excellence with comprehensive personal development.

About the Lavasa

Lavasa is India's first planned hill city, located in the scenic Western Ghats of Maharashtra, about 60 kilometers from Pune. Designed to offer a harmonious blend of urban living with nature, Lavasa is spread across lush green valleys and serene lakes, providing a tranquil and picturesque environment. The city is inspired by the principles of New Urbanism, which emphasize walkable neighborhoods, sustainable living, and a strong sense of community. Lavasa is not just a residential hub; it is a vibrant destination known for its infrastructure, recreational facilities, and opportunities for outdoor activities.

The city features a variety of amenities including hotels, resorts, adventure sports, and wellness centers, making it a popular destination for tourists and residents alike. In addition to its natural beauty and modern amenities, Lavasa has become a center for education and innovation, hosting prestigious institutions like CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Pune Lavasa Campus. The city's serene and focused environment makes it an ideal setting for academic pursuits and personal growth. Lavasa’s strategic location, away from the hustle and bustle of city life, yet close to major urban centers like Pune and Mumbai, offers a perfect balance for those seeking quality education in a peaceful setting.
